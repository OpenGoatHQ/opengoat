# Outbound & Sales-Led

Operators for modern outbound: cold email at scale (with infra knowledge), Clay-era enrichment workflows, founder-led sales motion, outbound-inbound bridge.

## What we look for

- Reply rate / meeting-booked numbers from real campaigns
- Comfort with the modern stack (Clay, Apollo, Smartlead, Instantly)
- Understanding of where outbound dies (almost always offer/list, not copy)

## Anti-fits

- Anyone running cold email without deliverability infra
- "Sales coaches" with no recent outbound experience
- People who promise specific meeting numbers cold

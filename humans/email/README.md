# Email

Operators for email at scale: lifecycle/behavioral email, cold email infrastructure (deliverability, domain warming, SPF/DKIM/DMARC), newsletter sponsorships, and welcome/reactivation sequences.

## What we look for

- Real deliverability numbers from past projects
- Knowledge of provider differences (Customer.io, Loops, Resend, Postmark, Iterable)
- Comfort with both creative (sequences) and infra (auth, sending pools)

## Anti-fits

- Anyone selling generic "open rate boosting" tricks
- Cold email as bot networks (we don't list spammers)

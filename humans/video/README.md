# Video

Operators who run video distribution: YouTube (long-form, Shorts), TikTok, Reels, scripting, hook engineering, retention editing, channel strategy.

## What we look for

- Owned channel results or named brand work
- Understanding of platform-specific algorithms (YT vs TikTok vs Reels are different)
- Comfort with both creative direction and analytics

## Anti-fits

- Agencies that just run paid promotion (that's `paid/`)
- "Viral coaches" with no public proof

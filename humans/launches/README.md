# Launches

Operators for product launches: Product Hunt, Hacker News (Show HN), BetaList, Indie Hackers, waitlists, re-launches, and milestone marketing.

## What we look for

- Operators who've shipped Top 5 launches on PH or front-page Show HN
- Understanding of the modern Twitter-led launch motion
- Honest about which launches are right for which products

## Anti-fits

- "Launch coaches" whose only proof is their own product
- Anyone who promises a specific PH ranking
- Bot-driven launch services

# Paid

Operators for paid acquisition: search ads (Google, Bing), paid social (Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok, Reddit), newsletter sponsorships, and influencer/creator partnerships.

## What we look for

- Verifiable ad accounts they've managed (named brands or anonymized)
- Spend range they're comfortable with (clear in profile)
- Honest about CAC realities, not LTV/CAC fantasies

## Anti-fits

- "Performance marketers" with no platform specialization
- Anyone promising specific ROAS without seeing the product

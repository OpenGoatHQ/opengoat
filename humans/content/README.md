# Content

Operators who build distribution through writing: blog strategy, founder-led media, ghostwriting, newsletter growth, podcasting, editorial systems.

## What we look for

- Public writing samples (theirs or ghostwritten with permission)
- Track record on a specific channel (Substack, blog, LinkedIn, Twitter)
- Distinct point of view, not generic copy

## Anti-fits

- "Content strategists" with no shipped writing
- AI-generated drafts as portfolio
- People who confuse content with copywriting

# Community

Operators who build community-led growth: Discord, Slack, niche forums (HN, Indie Hackers, Lobsters), and customer advocacy programs.

For Reddit specifically, see [`reddit/`](../reddit/).

## What we look for

- Real community they've built or scaled (named, with metrics)
- Understanding of mod dynamics, content cadence, member activation
- Distinct from "social media management"

## Anti-fits

- People who confuse community with audience
- Anyone proposing engagement bots

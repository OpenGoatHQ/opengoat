# Product-Led Growth

Operators who design self-serve growth: onboarding flows, activation metrics, viral loops, network effects, freemium mechanics, and self-serve to sales-assisted handoffs.

## What we look for

- Specific PLG experience at named B2B SaaS
- Comfort with both qualitative onboarding work and quantitative funnels
- Understanding of where PLG fails (usually retention, not acquisition)

## Anti-fits

- "PLG strategists" with only theoretical experience
- People who think PLG = freemium
